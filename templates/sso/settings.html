About SSO

Single Sign-On (SSO) allows you to log in using your existing Google or Microsoft account.

Benefits:
  • No need to remember another password
  • Faster login experience
  • Enhanced security with provider's 2FA
  • Automatic session management
Security:

Your password from Google/Microsoft is never shared with CaseHub. We only receive your email and profile information.
